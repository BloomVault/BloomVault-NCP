About BloomVault Farms

BloomVault Farms is rooted in Sutherlin, Virginia. We believe great results start with dependable plants and honest service. Our team keeps things simple and hands-on: clear communication, careful handling, and high standards from the first tray to the final delivery.

Our Mission

To provide healthy, uniform, flower-ready plants to licensed Virginia growers—backed by dependable timelines, straightforward pricing, and support you can count on.

Our Values

  • Quality without guesswork. Consistent, well-rooted starts that establish quickly and finish strong.
  • Dependable & on-time. Realistic timelines, clear updates, and follow-through—no runaround.
  • Compliance, made simple. Virginia requirements handled plainly, with tidy documentation.
  • Service with respect. We listen, we communicate, and we treat every order like it matters—because it does.
  • Always improving. We review every run and refine our process to keep raising the bar.

If you’re a licensed Virginia grower planning your next run—or trying something new—we’d love to help. We’re approachable, responsive, and excited to earn your trust. We look forward to working with you.
